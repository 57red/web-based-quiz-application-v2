Instructions

  1. Launching the Quiz:
    Open the quiz application on your device.
  2. Answering a Question:
    - Read the question carefully.
    - Select the correct answer from the provided options.
    - Click on your chosen answer.
  3. Navigating Questions:
    - Use the "Next" button to move to the next question.
    - Use the "Back" button to go back to the previous question.
    - You can navigate freely between questions.
  4. End of Quiz:
    - After answering the last question, you'll be directed to the summary pane for a final review
  5. Reviewing Answers:
    - In the summary pane, you can see which questions you answered.
    - It indicates if question is NO ANSWER.
  6. Finalizing the Quiz:
    - You can navigate and return to questions for a final review by clicking the Back to Questions Button.
    - Once satisfied, click on the "Submit" button to end the quiz.
  7. Viewing Results:
    - After submission, the quiz application will display your score and grade percentage.

    8. Enjoy playing the quiz application!
