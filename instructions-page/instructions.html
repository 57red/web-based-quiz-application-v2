Instructions

  1. Launching the Quiz:
    Open the quiz application on your device.
  2. Answering a Question:
    - Read the question carefully.
    - Select the correct answer from the provided options.
    - Click on your chosen answer.
  3. Navigating Questions:
    - Use the "Next" button to move to the next question.
    - Use the "Back" button to go back to the previous question.
    - You can navigate freely between questions.
  4. Flagging a Question:
    - If you are unsure about an answer, or want to review it later, use the "Flag" button.
    - The flagged questions will be saved, and you can revisit them in the summary pane.
  5. Reviewing Flagged Questions:
    - The summary pane will display a list of flagged questions.
    - Click on a flagged question to jump directly to it for review.
  6. End of Quiz:
    - After answering the last question, the quiz will end automatically.
    - You'll be directed to the summary pane for a final review.
  7. Reviewing Answers:
    - In the summary pane, you can see which questions you answered correctly and incorrectly.
    - Green indicates correct answers, while red indicates incorrect ones.
  8. Finalizing the Quiz:
    - You can navigate between flagged questions for a final review.
    - Once satisfied, click on the "Submit" button to end the quiz.
  9. Viewing Results:
    - After submission, the quiz application will display your score and a summary of your performance.
    - You may see correct/incorrect counts and a percentage.

    10. Enjoy playing the quiz application!
